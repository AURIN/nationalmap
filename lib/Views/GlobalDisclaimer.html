The AURIN Map website implements a geospatial map viewer which is intended for viewing over the internet. It is funded by the Australian Urban Research Infrastructure Network (AURIN), an e-infrastructure project made possible through the Commonwealth National Collaborative Research Infrastructure Strategy (NCRIS). The University of Melbourne, as AURIN's lead agent, administers, operates and controls AURIN. AURIN Map is copyright Â© The University of Melbourne 2011-2015.

The AURIN Map web-site is an extension of the Australian government's National Map initiative. It uses TerriaJS technologies developed for National Map, in collaboration with National ICT Australia Limited (NICTA), now part of Data 61.

The AURIN Map website provides access to datasets on urban infrastructure which have been provided by various third party data custodians.

As a condition of using this website, users should understand and must accept that the information and data provided on the AURIN Map website is:
